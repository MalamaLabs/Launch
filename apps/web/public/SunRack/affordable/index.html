Affordable Entry Points

Right-Size Your
Deployment

The solar and power infrastructure scales with each compute tier — panels and battery are sized to match the continuous GPU load. The AIO inverter and wiring topology are identical across all tiers, making in-place compute upgrades straightforward without redesigning the power system.

Once a community deploys any Nano node, upgrading compute later requires only adding panels and battery capacity — the inverter, wiring harness, and mounting infrastructure never change. The power system is an expandable permanent asset.

On Refurbished Hardware

EPYC server-grade CPUs are designed from the factory for 24/7 hyperscaler workloads. Units pulled from cloud data center rotations typically have low cumulative hours relative to their rated lifecycle, arrive with full BIOS and firmware support, and are available with 1–3 year warranties from certified resellers. For the Nano Core and Nano Pro tiers, refurb is the recommended path — not a cost-cut, but a smart sourcing strategy.
