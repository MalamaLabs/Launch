# Genesis Phase 1 Documentation Bundle

## Operator Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the Genesis Node operation infrastructure. By maintaining this hardware you secure a fundamental geographic hex zone.

1. **Preparation**: Ensure your deployment location has consistent solar exposure or an alternative power source, and is located securely.
2. **Mounting Setup**: The hardware form factor installs generally in under 30 minutes with out-of-the-box mounts. Ensure the sensor array maintains clear atmospheric exposure.
3. **App Synchronisation**: Utilize the Mālama Labs dApp Portal post-unboxing to link your hardware's unique ATECC slot ID to your Ethereum/Cardano address.

## Indigenous Stewardship Agreement (ISDA)

For mapped territories with overlapping self-identified Indigenous communities, your hardware contributes passively to direct decentralized funding. 15% (default) to up to 25% of node revenue is redirected at the smart contract level directly into registered steward DAOs. Settlement happens seamlessly via smart contracts natively in MLMA.
