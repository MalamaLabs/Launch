# Genesis 200 Phase 1 Timeline

## May 2026: Pre-sale Opens

- The Pre-sale officially begins. Participants can reserve their Genesis node allocation at the required $2k entry.
- Pre-qualification includes confirming region availability, capital allocation, and shipping constraints via the main portal.

## September 2026: Hardware Deployment

- Operator hardware shipments commence to respective locations.
- **Delivery and Setup:** Operators unbox, mount, and charge the secure solar-powered enclosure in an area with internet access.

## Late Sept - Early Oct 2026: Booting

- Setup and secure connection string booting takes ~30 minutes.
- The node connects to the network, and the initial 25% token allocation chunk vests instantly on first boot verify.

## Early-Mid October 2026: First Rewards Accrue

- Network metrics flow active across the DePIN.
- Validation checks process continuously.
- Regular 75% monthly vesting unlocks commence, and validators accumulate their validation token incentives.
