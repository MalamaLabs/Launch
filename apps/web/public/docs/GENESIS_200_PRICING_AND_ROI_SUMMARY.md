# Genesis 200 Pricing & ROI Summary

## Capital Requirement

The Genesis 200 node requires an upfront entry capital of **$2,000 USD**.

- **Hardware ($380):** Costs cover a Raspberry Pi Zero 2W, atmospheric sensors, weatherproof enclosure, solar UPS, and secure ATECC hardware enclave.
- **Geographic License ($1,620):** Grants operators exclusive rights to deploy inside a mapped contiguous geographic hex region.

## Illustrative Year 1 Earnings scenarios

*Assuming MLMA at $0.10-$0.30/token*

### 1. High Demand Hex (~50-100K validations/month)

- Monthly yield: ~50-100K MLMA
- **Payback:** Less than 1 week
- Year 1 Projected Total: $123k - $223k (at $0.20/MLMA)

### 2. Medium Demand Hex (~25-40K validations/month)

- Monthly yield: ~25-40K MLMA
- **Payback:** 1 - 4 weeks
- Year 1 Projected Total: $73k - $103k (at $0.20/MLMA)

### 3. Low Demand Hex (~8-15K validations/month)

- Monthly yield: ~8-15K MLMA
- **Payback:** 6 - 12 weeks
- Year 1 Projected Total: $39k - $52k (at $0.20/MLMA)

> **Important:** Earnings are fundamentally dependent on network data inflow in your specified Hex. Lower demand implies slower payback times. Typical volumes may be initially lower before sustained network stabilization.
